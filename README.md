## Project info

Have you ever had issues tracking your finances as a busy college student? Well, the Budget Buddy is here to help! The Budget Buddy is a budgeting app marketed towards busy college students in need of some help. The budget will include common expenses for students such as food, housing, entertainment, and savings. Simply fill in your information prompted by the app and get your monthly budget! You can also input feedback for the AI model to change and construct your personalized budget around exactly what you need!
